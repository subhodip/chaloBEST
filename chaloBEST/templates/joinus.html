ChaloBEST - Join Us

We are an open partnership project, mostly based on voluntary contribution. Anyone who wants to commit time to this project and supports open source development can contact us for more information.

Our team ranges from people with a passion for public transport, for open source development, for public activism, for database crunching, social entrepreneurship and for making stuff work.

There are some skills that we need urgently, if you have any of those please contact us at the earliest:

Travelers - (application testers) -

We always need people who travel by bus (and later also train) and who can help evaluating and improving the data and the usability of the ChaloBEST tools.

- Period of main activity: September 2012 onwards.

User Testers

Anyone who has experience taking ethnographic and usability interviews/tests is requested to contact us at the earliest. We are planning a larger scale research using our SMS, Android and website applications for the next round of improvements.

- Period of main activity: September to November 2012

Developers

- Period of main activity: September 2012 onwards.

Open Trip Planner / Java Developers

- Period of main activity: September 2012 onwards.
